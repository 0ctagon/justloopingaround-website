Marc Rebillet Live Stream Tierlist

My humble Tierlist of all the songs of Marc Rebillet Live Streams.

MENU

This site that lists all the songs Marc has done in his live streams since he started streaming on YouTube.
It has (almost) all the streams from Youtube and Twitch, totaling over a thousand songs.

Each song has a title, a timestamped link to the song in the stream, a rank, its (not rigorous, sometimes far-fetched) genre, its tempo, its length, a little comment I had while listening to the song and finally the chorus looped in the song (if there is one), which is really handy to find the song stuck in your head.

The ranking of the songs is a mix between my own biased appreciation and the number of times I listened to them over and over again. This ranking is purely arbitrary and doesn't really matter, a B+ for me might be an S level for you (although I think I did a good job giving S and A levels to very good songs, but that's open to debate). In any case, I encourage you to listen to all the songs because they all have something special.

A version of the site free of all bias is available on the page No-Tierlist.

To better find your way around the multitude of songs available, the site offers:

  • a Hall of fame listing my favorite songs,
  • a Jukebox which gives a random song, extremely practical for discovering songs,
  • a Song Explorer that allows you to find any song thanks to a search bar,
  • the list of all live shows sorted by release order.

You can also find a page of detailed statistics, analyzing the ranking system and some of the properties of the songs.

Happy discoveries!

Hall of Fame

All S tier (and A+) songs deserve their place in this Hall of Fame. Here is a small non-exhaustive selection of live songs that I listen to all the time depending on a certain mood. The selection is heavily biased towards songs from the period 2019-2020, which corresponds to the moment I was listening to Marc the most. This list is not fixed and will be updated over time.
