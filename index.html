Marc Rebillet Live Stream Repertoire

Repertoire of songs created by Marc Rebillet during live streams.

MENU

This website lists all the songs Marc has done in his live streams since he started streaming on YouTube.
It has (almost) all the streams from Youtube and Twitch, totaling over a thousand songs.

A tierlist of all the songs with my biased opinion is available on the Tierlist page.

You can also find a page of detailed statistics, analyzing the ranking system and some of the properties of the songs.

To better find your way around the multitude of songs available, the site offers:

  • Stream highlights section listing a few famous/nice songs to start with,
  • a Jukebox which gives a random song, extremely practical for discovering songs,
  • a Song Explorer that allows you to find any song thanks to a search bar,
  • the list of all live shows sorted by release order.

Happy discoveries!
