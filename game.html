How to play

Each day once a day you can guess a song that Marc made during a stream by listening to short extracts.

Click on the play button to listen to a 15-second clip starting at the beginning of the song.

Make a guess by typing and selecting a song or skip to unlock the next clip, which will start at 20% (then 40%, 60% and 80%) of the song.

Guessed songs are displayed below the guess bar. Each recap can be clicked to open the stream's page.

Small hints are provided: words in green indicate that the highlighted information is also in the song of the day; ▲ and ▼ indicate if the song's length is longer or shorter than your guess, and if it was played in a future or previous stream.

Description of the icons from the guessed song recap:

Genre Length Choral Tempo Stream Title Stream Media Stream Date Stream with or without public

You can make as many guesses as you want, the game will end when you guess the song correctly or if you concede using the flag button.

Good luck and have fun!
